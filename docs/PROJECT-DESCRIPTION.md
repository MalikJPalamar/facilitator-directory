# The Directory

## One-liner

The Directory gives a certification school's graduates one beautiful, trustworthy home where the right people — and their AI assistants — can actually find them.

## Short pitch

The Directory turns your certified facilitators into a branded, searchable, verified community in one place. Every graduate gets a real, photo-led profile with a verified badge that says *this person is certified through us*. Visitors filter by modality, location, and approach — and find the right facilitator in seconds.

## The problem

Certified facilitators are scattered across personal sites, social handles, and word of mouth — easy to lose, hard to vet. Someone looking for the right practitioner has no reliable way to tell who's actually trained and trustworthy. And the schools that did the training have no branded home for the graduates who carry their work into the world.

## What it is

The Directory is an AI-native platform that turns a certification school's graduates into a branded, searchable, verified directory. It's the front door for your community: each facilitator gets a full profile — their story, their approach, the modalities they're trained in, their certification, and a direct way to reach out — all carrying a verified badge that ties their credibility back to the school. It's instantly searchable by modality, location, and the way someone practices, so the right facilitators surface in a second. And because every profile is structured and machine-readable, the same community that people can search is one that AI assistants can read and recommend.

## Who it's for

**Schools**
Your own branded home for the graduates who carry your work forward. You decide who's verified, and that badge becomes the trust signal that sets your community apart. It feels like you, because it is you.

**Practitioners & facilitators**
A real, photo-led profile that shows your story, your modalities, your certification, and a way for people to reach you directly — verified by the school that trained you. When you're certified, you get to claim your profile and be found.

**AI agents**
This is the differentiator. Profiles aren't just web pages — they're structured and machine-readable through MCP and JSON-LD. As more people ask assistants like ChatGPT or Claude to *"find me a breathwork facilitator,"* your community is built to actually show up in that answer.

## Why it's different

We treat agents as customers, not an afterthought: the directory is designed to be queried by AI assistants, so your facilitators surface in the answers people increasingly trust. Every profile carries verified-by-the-school trust — credibility that comes from the people who did the training, not a self-reported claim. And a nightly AI insight loop quietly learns from how the directory is actually used, turning real activity into better visibility for your community over time.
